Social Media Campaign — Cancer Research UK

For this group project, my team and I created a social media campaign for Cancer Research UK to raise awareness and promote a community fundraising event — a charity walk from Twickenham Stadium to Bushy Park.

We set up our own Instagram accounts (@simroy.for.cr_uk) to establish a digital presence, document campaign milestones, invite participation, and share key visuals and messages with our audience. The campaign included awareness posts, an invitation announcement, and promotional content leading up to the walk.

Instagram was chosen as the primary platform due to its visual nature and strong engagement potential for community-based initiatives. It allowed us to create a consistent visual identity and communicate key campaign messages clearly.

Live account: instagram.com/simroy.for.cr_uk

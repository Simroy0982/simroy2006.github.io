Motion Design Countdown

University Project · Adobe After Effects

Concept Storyboard Techniques Assets Final Video

Concept & Idea

This project focused on creating a dynamic 10–1 countdown that explores rhythm, pacing, and visual impact inspired by cinematic motion graphics.

A mood board was developed to define colour, typography, and motion style before production began.

Storyboard

The storyboard mapped out transitions and timing for each number, ensuring a smooth and engaging countdown sequence.

Techniques Used

Adobe After Effects was used to animate typography, shapes, and transitions. Keyframing, easing, motion blur, and pre-compositions were applied to enhance smoothness and flow.

Assets

This project uses a combination of externally sourced assets and original assets created specifically for the animation.

Assets from Tutorials & Online Resources

Some visual elements were recreated by following online tutorials to better understand professional motion design workflows. These assets were modified and adapted to fit the overall visual style of the project.

Assets

Assets Created by Me

Original assets including some photos and other elements were designed from scratch to personalise the animation and strengthen its visual identity.

Final Outcome

The final result is a 10-second animated countdown demonstrating strong control of timing, and pacing.
